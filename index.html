Countering Misinformation Reading Group

"No investigation, no right to speak!"

About

85% of people worry about disinformation. Without access to reliable information, the potential for positive societal change is diminished and future generations are put at risk.

We engage in critical discussions to understand the truth of the issues that make up today's political problems. The ultimate goal of the group is for each person to come to their own conclusions about how to engage with these realities effectively.
